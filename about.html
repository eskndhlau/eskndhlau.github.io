About Elizabeth’s African Wholesome Vegan Food

With the growing popularity of veganism in the last decade it has become increasingly clear that there’s a need to share the delicious and wonderful wonders and options of food that one can indulge in. The meals are cheap, easy and quick and also ideal for busy college students and even busier mum’s who need quick and healthy meals for their young ones. This recipe book is just the right thing for someone who’s new to veganism, and non-vegans who would just like to have a taste of a vegan meal with an Southern African twist. Elizabeth’s African Wholesome Vegan Food, is the place vegan and plant-based enthusiasts can easily find quick, easy to follow and tasty recipes that won’t break your budget. This website contains images showcasing the step by step process of each recipe to ensure that you create the perfect delightful meal; a list of all the important ingredients; preparation and cook times to help you organise your days well!

The recipe book contains appetizers, soups, salads, main dishes, sides, snacks, wholesome cakes and deserts. The book also contains delicious grill recipes that everyone will enjoy including non-vegans. People can also try any of my quick yet enjoyable vegan-friendly recipes especially when one is planning a barbecue and yet they have guests that are vegan and healthy eaters.

Though this recipe book seems to emphasise a safe haven for vegans and plant-based enthusiasts, remember everyone is welcome!

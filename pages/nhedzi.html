Nhedzi Soup

Share |
Print

Ingridients

  • 2 cups Nhedzi mushroom, chopped
  • 1 cup thin green beans
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • ½ cup water, 2 tablespoons cream of mushroom flavouring
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Preparation

  • Start by frying the mushrooms and green beans in a pan with the butter, olive oil and crushed garlic.
  • When the mushrooms and the green beans start to become tender, reduce the heat and let them cook for another 2 minutes.
  • After 2 minutes, slowly add the water to the mushroom and vegetable mix and let simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Once the has thickened ever so slightly, remove the mushroom and vegetable mix from the stove and flavour with salt and black pepper.
  • Serve the mushroom and veggie mixture over fluffy white rice or even with pasta.

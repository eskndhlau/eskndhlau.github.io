Tea & Chimodho

Share |

OVERVIEW

  • 1 Tea Bag of Rooibos
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Lemon

HOW TO MAKE ROOIBOS TEA

  • Medium heat water in a medium sized pot. Once the water has boiled, turn off the heat.
  • Now pour the rooibos tea in a teapot. Pour the boiling water over the tea. Leave it to stand for 5 to 6 minutes, so that the flavour of rooibos tea is fully infused with the hot water.
  • Pour the prepared tea into teacups. Sweeten it by adding sugar cubes.
  • Add a few drops of lemon juice for flavour.
  • Serve the hot tea and enjoy!

HOW TO MAKE CHIMODHO

Ingridients

  • 1 cup sweet potato or cassava flour,
  • 1 cup maize/ sorghum or millet flour
  • 1 cup wheat flour
  • 1-heaped teaspoon salt
  • Half a teaspoon baking soda
  • Water as required
  • Sugar to taste

Method

  • Mix all of the flours, salt, soda and sugar in bowl
  • Add in the water, to get a soft dough
  • Grease the walls of a pot with oil, and then shape the dough inside the pot
  • 1-heaped teaspoon salt
  • Bake the bread till browned.

MAIZE PORRIDGE WITH DOVI AND MILK

Share |
Print

30 - 45 MINS | SERVES 6

  • 250 ml (1 cups) cold water
  • 250 ml (1 cup) Pride maize meal
  • 1.25 L (5 cups) water
  • 5 ml (1 tsp) salt

Preparation

  • Mix together maize meal with cold water to form a sludge.
  • Boil remaining water and salt in a heavy-based pot with a lid.
  • Over medium to low heat, stir maize meal mixture into the boiling water. Cover with a lid then simmer for at least 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Serving Suggestion:

Ideal for breakfast. Serve with milk, butter (for non-vegans), and sugar or sweetener and peanut butter.

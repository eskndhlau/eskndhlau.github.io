CHAKALAKA

Share |
Print

Ingridients

  • 1 cup corn (if the corn is too dry you may soak it overnight)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 large onion
  • 1/2 green pepper>
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 3 carrots
  • 2 large chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tin baked bean s
  • salt and pepper to taste + seasoning (we used garlic and herb seasoning)

Preparation

  • Heat oil in a large pan
  • Fry onion and green pepper
  • Once onions are translucent, add curry powder and fry for a minute
  • Add carrots and fry for a minute
  • Stir in tomatoes and season with salt and pepper
  • Cover pot with lid and allow to boil for 5 minutes
  • Add 1 tin baked beans and season with your seasoning, stir well
  • Cover pan with lid and allow to simmer for 10 minutes
